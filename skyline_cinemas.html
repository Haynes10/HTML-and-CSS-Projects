Accommodations

Located in the heart of Toronto, our cinema is designed with your comfort and accessibility in mind.

We offer spacious, cushioned seating, including accessible spots for guests using wheelchairs.

Our venue is fully compliant with AODA standards, with barrier-free entrances, accessible washrooms, and assistive listening devices available upon request.

Whether you're catching the latest blockbuster or enjoying a quiet matinee, we make it our priority to ensure every guest feels welcome and well cared for.
